GharBari - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What products do you offer?

We offer a wide range of fresh vegetables and grocery items. You can explore our product catalog on the app.

2. How can I place an order?

To place an order, simply log in to your account, browse the products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your order.

3. Is there a minimum order amount?

Yes, we have a minimum order amount to ensure efficient delivery. The minimum order requirement may vary, and you will be notified during the checkout process.

4. How can I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS. You can use this link to track the status of your delivery in real-time.

5. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and cash on delivery. Please check the available payment options during the checkout process.

6. How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team by calling +9779702627315 or emailing customersupport@gharbari.com. We are available to assist you during our business hours.

7. Can I modify or cancel my order?

Once an order is confirmed, modifications may not be possible. For cancellations, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible, and we will assist you based on the order status.

8. How do I return a product?

For information on returning products, please refer to our Return Policy.

9. Do you offer discounts or promotions?

Yes, we regularly offer discounts and promotions. Keep an eye on our app and subscribe to our newsletter to stay informed about the latest deals.
